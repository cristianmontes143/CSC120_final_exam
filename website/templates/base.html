What is Intelligent Systems?

Intelligent systems refer to computer-based systems that can autonomously perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as perception, reasoning, learning, and decision-making. These systems often leverage techniques from artificial intelligence, machine learning, and other related fields to achieve their goals.

Read More

Linear Regression

Linear regression is a supervised learning algorithm used to predict continuous output variables.

Logistic Regression

Logistic regression is a supervised learning algorithm used to predict binary or categorical output variables based on input variables.

Neural Network

Neural networks are a class of algorithms inspired by the structure and function of the human brain, used for a wide range of tasks such as image recognition, natural language processing, and more.

Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement learning is a type of machine learning where an agent learns to make decisions in an environment, based on trial-and-error learning and feedback in the form of rewards or penalties.

Deep Learning

Deep learning is a subset of neural networks that involves training multi-layered models to perform complex tasks, often with large amounts of data.

Models

These are the models that we created for CSC120

Linear Regression

Our Linear Regression model utilizes mortgage interest rates to predict median house prices. By training the model with 16 carefully selected data points, we have developed a powerful tool for various applications. Whether you're interested in real estate investment, mortgage lending, government policy-making, or economic analysis, our trained model provides valuable insights to support your decision-making process.

Logistic Regression

Our innovative personal loan predictor uses Logical Regression to determine whether an individual is likely to be approved for a loan. By analyzing a vast dataset of 5000 records, our model has been trained to accurately forecast loan outcomes. With this tool, you can streamline the loan application process and receive tailored loan offers that suit your unique financial needs.

Get Started

Neural Network

Our state-of-the-art neural network model has been specifically designed to assess the likelihood of a heart attack. Trained on a comprehensive dataset of 300 records, this powerful tool can provide you with an estimated probability of experiencing a heart attack. However, please note that this model is intended for code demonstration purposes only and should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional for accurate medical assessments.

Get Started

Deep Learning

Our advanced deep learning system harnesses the power of the BERT model to analyze the sentiment of tweets. With a robust training dataset comprising 16,200 unique records, our model is adept at detecting sentiment across a wide range, from very negative to very positive. Whether you're interested in gauging public opinion, conducting market research, or monitoring brand sentiment, our trained model provides accurate and insightful sentiment analysis for your Twitter data

Get Started

Reinforcement Learning

Our groundbreaking approach to reinforcement learning involves training an AI using a q-table within a grid-world traffic simulation. This trained AI offers tremendous potential for a range of applications, including autonomous cars, traffic management, and road construction/management. With our cutting-edge technology, you can unlock innovative solutions for optimizing traffic flow, improving road safety, and enhancing overall transportation efficiency. Experience the future of intelligent traffic systems with our trained AI.
